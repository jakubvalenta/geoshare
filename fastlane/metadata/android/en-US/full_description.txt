Share a map link with Geo Share and the app will open it in another installed map app.

<b>Supported map links</b>

- Google Maps
- Apple Maps
- Amap (AutoNavi)
- CoMaps
- HERE WeGo
- Magic Earth
- Maps.me
- Mapy.com
- OpenStreetMap
- Organic Maps
- OsmAnd
- Waze
- Yandex Maps
- coordinates

<b>Example use cases</b>

When someone sends you a Google Maps link, but you prefer using OpenStreetMap, you can quickly <b>open the same location</b> in OsmAnd or Organic Maps.

When you like Google Maps for finding places, but you prefer a different app for navigation, you can easily switch from Google Maps to your favorite <b>navigation app</b>.

<b>Other features</b>

- Shows the <br>geographic coordinates</b> of a map link and allows copying them to clipboard in various formats, for example as a geo: link.
- Allows choosing an action to perform <br>automatically</b> when a map link is converted to coordinates.
- Allows <br>launching navigation</b> in selected apps with one tap.
- Retries repeatedly when the <br>internet connection</b> is patchy.
- Allows <br>pasting map links</b> directly into the app, instead of sharing them with it.
- Has an interface that adapts to <br>tablets</b>.

<b>How it works and privacy considerations</b>

Geo Share converts map links (e.g. https://maps.app.goo.gl/...) into geo: links that can be opened by other map apps. To create a geo: link, geographic coordinates are required. Geo Share extracts them from the map URL.

However, not all map URLs include coordinates. In such cases, Geo Share will <b>prompt you for permission to connect to the map service (Google Maps, Apple Maps etc.)</b> and retrieve the coordinates from either HTTP headers or the HTML document of the link.
